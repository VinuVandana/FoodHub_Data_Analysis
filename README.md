# FoodHub Data Analysis

## Business Context

The number of restaurants in New York is increasing day by day. Lots of students and busy professionals rely on those restaurants due to their hectic lifestyles. An online food delivery service is a great option for them. It provides them with good food from their favorite restaurants.

FoodHub, a food aggregator company, offers access to multiple restaurants through a single smartphone app. The app allows restaurants to receive direct online orders from customers. The app assigns a delivery person from the company to pick up the order after it is confirmed by the restaurant.

### How It Works

1. The delivery person uses the map to reach the restaurant and waits for the food package
2. Once the food package is handed over to the delivery person, they confirm the pick-up in the app
3. The delivery person travels to the customer's location to deliver the food
4. The delivery person confirms the drop-off in the app after delivering the food package
5. The customer can rate the order in the app
6. FoodHub earns money by collecting a fixed margin of the delivery order from restaurants

## Objective

The food aggregator company has stored the data of different orders made by registered customers in its online portal. They want to analyze the data to get a fair idea about the demand for different restaurants, which will help them enhance their customer experience.

As a Data Scientist hired by this company, the Data Science team has shared key questions that need to be answered. This project performs data analysis to find answers to these critical business questions that will help the company improve its business operations and customer experience.

## Project Focus

- Analyze restaurant demand patterns
- Understand customer ordering behavior
- Identify opportunities for business improvement
- Enhance customer experience based on data insights
- Support decision-making with data-driven recommendations
